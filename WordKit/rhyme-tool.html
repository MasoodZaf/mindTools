About anagrams
What's an anagram?
A word formed by rearranging all letters of another word. LISTEN = SILENT = ENLIST = TINSEL.
Anagram vs unscramble
Anagrams use ALL letters. Unscrambling finds words using any subset of the letters.

Rhyme types

Near rhyme

Slant rhyme
